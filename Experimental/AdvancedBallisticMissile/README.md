**Experimental**

An experimental ballistic missile mod, adding terminal propulsion and terminal maneuvering capabilities. Impact speed can reach 15,000 KM/H, with 100G terminal maneuvers.

This ballistic missile is unstoppable even by a hundred R9s.

The terminal maneuvering logic has been improved. Once terminal propulsion is triggered, the missile will consistently change direction every 10 seconds and perform a sharp turn to strike the target at 10 km distance.

Unknown bugs may exist, please use with caution.
